Project ideas

●Overview

I want to make a language learning app, it allows students to learn English, this app can listen to different words in English pronunciation, or teach students some commonly used sentences, still can add listening game in the app, according to hear words of choosing the right words. Of course, if you have any questions, you can also leave a message or ask professional people on the app, because the mobile phone is a product of People's Daily carry, I think in this good way can let people to learn English

●Motivation

English is one of the most popular language in the world now, so English learning is necessary, however, not all people can pay a lot for learning English tuition, so to launch a free English learning app is feasible, a large number of listening is the best way to learn English, so we can use the phone anytime and anywhere to hear English words and sentences, this not only saves money, makes English learning as simple, what you need is just a mobile phone.

●Description

I'll explain some of the main parts of the app below

●convenient

With the development of science and technology, the performance of people's mobile phones is getting better and better, so we can do a lot of things with mobile phones. Therefore, learning on mobile phones makes people no longer need pen and paper, and gets rid of the regular way of learning. This kind of learning method becomes cooler and popular with young people.

●Communication

There are a lot of students who don't learn English well because they can't find professional people to solve the problems they meet.I think the best way to solve the problem is to ask, so we will add a message and comment function in the app, We will also add online chat Windows where you can communicate with others online, send messages and pictures. when you encounter any problems in English, you can leave a message on it, or start chart with other online, and professional people will answer your questions

●Sharing

On this app, you can share how you learn English with others,This provides more references for many English learners, and people can share some learning materials about English.

●Improve students' interest in learning

As mobile phones are highly entertaining, many students use them to play mobile games, listen to music and watch movies. Therefore, we will add some game elements to the English learning app, so that learning English will not become so boring, and people will not easily turn off and uninstall the app.
